Built on a job site, not in a boardroom.

Running a crew means you already know your work is good — what you don't always have is the time to call every lead back in the first five minutes, every time.

So we built the thing that does it for you: AI-powered scoring and instant alerts, so the fastest response is always yours.

Speed is a fairness issue.
