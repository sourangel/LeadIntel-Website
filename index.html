Every contractor has this leak. It's invisible on a spreadsheet and brutal on a P&L — a good lead doesn't get rejected, it just gets ignored until it's gone.

STAGE 01

Lead comes in

A homeowner fills out a form or texts your number at 9:47pm on a Tuesday.

● FRESH
STAGE 02

● DELAYED
