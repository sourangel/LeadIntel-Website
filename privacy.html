Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

LeadIntel LLC ("LeadIntel CRM," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have, when you use our website and our software-as-a-service platform (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly — such as your name, business name, email address, and phone number when you submit a demo request, sign up for the Service, or contact us.
  • Information from your customers — when contractors use the Service, their customers (end leads) submit project details, contact information, and other inputs through our intake chatbot or forms.
  • Usage data — including pages visited, time spent on the site, browser type, device information, and general analytics collected automatically when you use our website or dashboard.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — we use cookies to operate the website, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service, including lead scoring, alerts, and the dashboard
  • Communicate with you about your account, demo requests, and support questions
  • Send SMS or email alerts you've requested as part of the Service
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements

3. SMS and Text Messaging

If you opt in to receive text message alerts as part of the Service, your phone number is used solely to deliver those alerts. We do not sell or share phone numbers collected for SMS purposes with third parties for marketing purposes. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of SMS alerts at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. See our Terms of Service for full SMS program details.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the Service (such as our automation, database, email, and SMS infrastructure providers)
  • Legal authorities when required by law, or to protect our rights, safety, or property
  • A successor business in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business purposes, such as maintaining business records or complying with legal obligations.

6. Your Choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the email below. You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You can decline cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not function properly without them.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll post the updated version on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
